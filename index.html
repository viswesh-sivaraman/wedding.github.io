How to Reach Lagoona Resort Lonavala

  • By Car (from Mumbai): Take the Mumbai-Pune Expressway (NH48) towards Pune. Exit at Lonavala, then follow signs to Tungarli Road. Lagoona Resort is approximately 90 km from Mumbai, a 1.5–2-hour drive. Parking is available at the resort.
  • By Car (from Pune): Drive via the Mumbai-Pune Expressway (NH48) towards Mumbai. Exit at Lonavala and head to Tungarli Road. The resort is about 60 km from Pune, a 1–1.5-hour drive.
  • By Train: Take a train to Lonavala Railway Station (LNL) from Mumbai (e.g., Intercity Express, ~2 hours) or Pune (~1 hour). From the station, hire a taxi or auto-rickshaw to Lagoona Resort (5–10 minutes, ~3 km).
  • By Flight: Fly to Pune International Airport (PNQ, ~70 km) or Mumbai’s Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport (BOM, ~90 km). From either airport, hire a taxi or book a cab (e.g., Ola/Uber) to Lagoona Resort. Travel time is 1.5–2 hours from Pune or 2–2.5 hours from Mumbai.
  • Address: Lagoona Resort, S.No. 55, Tungarli, Lonavala, Maharashtra 410403, India.
